Auto Repair for Columbia City, Oregon Drivers

Columbia City sits right next door to our St. Helens shop — for most of the town, we're closer than the grocery store run you already make.

Call (503) 867-0609

The Closest Thing to a Shop in Town

Columbia City is a quiet riverfront town without a full-service repair shop of its own — and it doesn't really need one, because we're a few minutes down the road. Drop your car with us in the morning and the logistics that make repairs painful elsewhere just don't apply: no arranging a day of rides, no leaving your vehicle in another city, no planning your week around the repair.

That closeness changes the relationship, too. When we call with what we found, you can swing by and look at it on the lift yourself before deciding. We'd rather show you than tell you.

Small-Town Service, Literally Next Door

Between the highway and the river, Columbia City is the kind of place where people know their neighbors — and we're effectively one of them. Our customers from Columbia City get the same deal as everyone who walks in: a clear diagnosis, an estimate you approve before work starts, and a finished job when we said it would be finished. The difference is you barely have to leave town to get it.
