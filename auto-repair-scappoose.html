Auto Repair for Scappoose, Oregon Drivers

Scappoose is right down the road from our shop, close enough that we regularly pick up customers' cars ourselves and bring them in for repair.

Call (503) 867-0609

We'll Come Get Your Car

A good number of our regulars are Scappoose drivers, and for many of them the repair starts with us driving to their driveway. Scappoose is only a few minutes down Highway 30 from our St. Helens shop, so when a car won't start, or its owner just can't spare the trip, we pick it up, do the work, and get it back. Call ahead and we'll arrange it.

Built for Commuter Miles

Scappoose runs on the Highway 30 commute. Most working households here point a car at Portland every morning, and those daily miles add up faster than almost any other kind of driving. Think heat cycles, stop-and-go wear, and the slow grind that turns small problems into roadside ones.

A commuter car doesn't get sick days. When yours starts making a noise on the way home, you need it diagnosed honestly and fixed on schedule, because Thursday morning it has to make the drive again. That's the job we've set up our shop to do: a clear diagnosis, an upfront estimate you approve first, and the car ready when we said it would be.

Same Town Feel, One Exit Up

Scappoose and St. Helens are cut from the same cloth. Scappoose is just a little smaller. We've done all sorts of work for Scappoose customers over the years, and for most of them we're closer than anything Portland offers and a lot more personal. You deal with the owners, you get a straight answer, and your money stays in the county.
