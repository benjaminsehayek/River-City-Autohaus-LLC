We Show You What's Wrong. Then You Decide.

Everyone's heard the horror stories about mechanics — repairs that weren't needed, bills padded with mystery labor. Our fix for that is simple: evidence. We take pictures of what we find on your vehicle and show you exactly what's wrong before we quote anything. No needless repairs, no scare tactics — just the problem, the fix, and the price.

Proof, Not Pressure

Photos of the actual worn or broken part on your actual car, shown to you before you approve a dime of work.

A Shop Built for a Working County

Columbia County is blue-collar country — contractors hauling to job sites, commuters grinding Highway 30 into Portland every day, and trucks that earn their keep. Vehicles work hard out here, and there aren't many shops to go around. We proudly serve drivers in:

St. Helens

Our hometown — the shop is right here on Eilertson St.

Scappoose

Minutes down Highway 30 — we'll even pick your car up.

Rainier

Clatskanie
